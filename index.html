Hello! I am a PhD student under Prof Raul Longoria at UT Austin. I'm currently investigating how to use data provided by an implantable heart pump (Left Ventricular Assist Device) to estimate physiological parameters of the patient. My work involves model based techniques such as Kalman Filters, as well as data driven approaches using machine learning. We test these methods both in simulation, and in Hardware-in-the-Loop experiments.
